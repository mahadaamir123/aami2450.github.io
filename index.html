About

How Nobu Digital came to be.

  • September 7th, 2017

    Our Humble Beginnings

    The two founders met on this day in their CP 202 class. Both having a passion for empowering students through a strong digital presence they decided to work together and build an agency.

  • October 1st, 2017

    An Agency is Born

    After figuring out all the nitty gritty details it was finally time to execute. On this day Nobu Digital officially came into being. The founders focused on marketing their vision to students across Laurier with the goal to land their first client.

  • November 20st, 2017

    Transition to Full Service

    On this day Nobu Digital hit their 100th client. This was a very humbling day because the founders were running this agency while still in school persuing their degrees.

  • December 2nd, 2017

    Phase Two Expansion

    After seeing how many students they were able to help out the founders decided that next year they would try taking a huge step and expand to other universities across Ontario. This goal is still in progress and will be put into play beginning of 2018.
